About us

Ean@Home is an application and a profitless organization made by code enthusiasts ( MonkeyTime, MaxCo41, boubaSambare, derrynEdwards) to help people do their shopping during the Covid-19 health crisis. We help people at #staysafe #stayathome, as much as possible, by connecting customer orders, the products they need every day, to his stores or SME closest to him, via a barcode scanner instantaneous and in real time, which sends your request directly to the SME merchant closest to you, and by geolocation of the demand for your products. The client can request the best planning, which is managed by our application in accordance with the lockdown rules. In order to pick up their products from the shops of SME, at the time that suits them the most, or so the customer can request home delivery too.
