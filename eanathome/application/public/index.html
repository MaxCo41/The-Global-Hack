Ean@Home

We help people to #staysafe #stayathome (as possible) by connecting customer order (products needed) with its nearest SMEs through a real time instant barcode scanner who dispatch/send your demand directly to the SMEs merchant(s) nearest to you and by geolocation of your product demand. Customer can request the best scheduling (who is managed by our app who respect covid-19 crisis rules) to pickup their products to the shop/store of the SMEs at the best moment or customer can request a delivery at home
